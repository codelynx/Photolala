Keyboard Shortcuts

Master Photolala's keyboard shortcuts to work more efficiently. Most shortcuts follow platform conventions.

Note: On Windows and Linux, replace ⌘ (Command) with Ctrl.

Customizing Shortcuts

You can customize keyboard shortcuts in Preferences:

  1. Open Preferences (⌘, on Mac)
  2. Navigate to Keyboard tab
  3. Click on any shortcut to modify
  4. Press your new key combination
  5. Click Save to apply changes
Tip: Export your custom shortcuts to share across devices or backup your preferences.

Platform-Specific Notes

macOS

  • Touch Bar support for common actions (if available)
  • Force Touch for Quick Look preview
  • Trackpad gestures for zooming and navigation

iOS/iPadOS

  • External keyboard support for all shortcuts
  • Hold ⌘ key to see available shortcuts
  • iPad-specific multitasking gestures
